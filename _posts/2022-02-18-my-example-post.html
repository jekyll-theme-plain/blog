--- --- In the Plain Jekyll theme, [^1] posts appear as plain text but are served as text/html. Due to Jekyll limitations, post filenames must be in the format YYYY-MM-DD-title.html. [^2] No HTML tags are rendered in the Plain theme, however. [^1]: https://github.com/jekyll-theme-plain/jekyll-theme-plain [^2]: https://jekyllrb.com/docs/posts/
